Through My Lens, Every Frame Tells a Story

I'm Davies Tony, a passionate visual storyteller with a deep love for capturing raw, authentic moments. From the golden glow of weddings to the vibrant pulse of urban life, my work reflects a deep respect for emotion, detail, and light.

My photography journey has taken me across Zambia and beyond, working with award-winning artists, brands, and individuals who believe in the power of visual storytelling. Let’s create something timeless.
